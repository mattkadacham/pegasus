Our Story

A Gem in the
Heart of Canterbury

Founded in July 2018, Pegasus Tap Room is an upmarket, eco-friendly bar nestled on historic St Dunstan's Street — just a few hundred yards from Canterbury Cathedral. The venue combines an original Victorian character — exposed wooden beams, intimate alcoves, vintage cinema seats — with a world-class selection of drinks curated for the genuinely curious.

Whether you're a craft beer enthusiast chasing the next great IPA or a gin devotee exploring small-batch distilleries from around the globe, you'll find something extraordinary here.

What We Pour

The Finest Drops,
Rotating Daily

Our electronic blackboard keeps the line-up fresh. From UK microbreweries to Belgian farmhouse ales and Norwegian craft — every tap tells a story.

🍺 14
Craft Beer on Tap

Fourteen rotating handles sourced from celebrated UK and European breweries. Expect IPAs, lagers, stouts, sours, and seasonal specials from the likes of Kernel, Hammerton, and Pig & Porter.

🍸 100+
World Gins

Over one hundred gins from across the globe line the mirrored back bar, illuminated by moody lighting. Our team can guide you to your perfect serve — classic or adventurous.

🥃 50+
Premium Rum

From aged Caribbean rums to bold agricoles, our rum selection rivals the gin list. Ask a bartender for a guided flight through the collection.

🍷
Fine Wines

A curated wine selection to complement the bar. Approachable and interesting — from crisp whites to rich reds and everything in between.

🥤 2
Cider on Tap

Two craft ciders always on draught, selected to complement the rotating beer list with both traditional and modern styles.

📱
QR Menu

Browse our entire paperless drinks menu from your phone using our QR code at the bar — eco-friendly and always up to date.

The Space

Old Bones,
New Spirit

Step into a characterful building with centuries of Canterbury history. Original wooden beams frame intimate alcoves and a warm, dimly lit bar. Two pavement tables let you watch the world pass by on St Dunstan's Street.
